Returns & Refunds Policy

Return Window

We offer a 30-day return policy for all eligible items. The return window begins on the date you receive your order. To be eligible for a return, items must be:

  • Unused and in original packaging
  • Free from damage, stains, or odors
  • Accompanied by all original accessories and documentation

How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our support team at support@crannix.com with your order number
  2. Provide details about the item and reason for return
  3. Wait for return authorization and shipping instructions
  4. Package the item securely and ship it back

Please note: Returns without prior authorization will not be accepted.

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your return:

  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Original shipping costs are non-refundable
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • You'll receive email confirmation once the refund is processed

Exchange Options

We offer exchanges for different sizes or colors of the same product. To request an exchange, indicate your preference when initiating the return. If the requested exchange is unavailable, we'll issue a refund.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Items used or damaged after delivery
  • Items without original packaging or tags
  • Personal care items that have been opened
  • Items marked as final sale or clearance

Defective or Damaged Items

If you receive a defective or damaged item, please contact us immediately. We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund at no additional cost. Please include photos of the damage when contacting us.

Contact Us

For return questions or concerns, please contact us at support@crannix.com or call 1-800-CRANNIX.

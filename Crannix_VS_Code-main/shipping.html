Shipping Information

Shipping Options & Delivery Times

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs:

  • Standard Shipping (Free): 5-7 business days for orders over ₹4,150
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days - ₹1,078.17
  • Priority Shipping: 1-2 business days - ₹1,659.17

Orders placed before 2 PM IST ship same day (Monday-Friday). Orders placed after 2 PM IST will ship the next business day.

Free Shipping

Enjoy free standard shipping on all orders totaling ₹4,150 or more after discounts are applied. This offer is valid for standard shipping within the contiguous region.

International Shipping

We ship to over 100 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination:

  • Canada: ₹1,327.17 - 7-14 business days
  • UK & Europe: ₹2,072.17 - 10-21 business days
  • Australia & NZ: ₹2,489.17 - 14-28 business days
  • Other Countries: Contact us for rates

International orders may be subject to customs duties, taxes, and import fees which are the responsibility of the recipient.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with tracking information. You can also track your order on our Order Tracking page using your order number and email address.

Shipping Restrictions

We currently cannot ship to:

  • PO Boxes (for certain carriers)
  • APO/FPO military addresses
  • Some remote areas requiring special delivery

If your address is not serviceable, we'll contact you to arrange an alternative.

Lost or Delayed Packages

If your package is lost or significantly delayed, please contact our support team. We'll work with the carrier to resolve the issue and ensure you receive your order or a full refund.

Contact Us

For shipping questions or concerns, please contact us at support@crannix.com or call 1-800-CRANNIX.
